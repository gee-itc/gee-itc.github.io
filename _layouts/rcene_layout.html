International Conference on Thermofluids & The Regional Conference on Energy Engineering

The Department of Mechanical and Industrial Engineering of Gadjah Mada University in collaboration with AUN/SEED-NET and JICA proudly present the 2015 Regional Conference on Energy Engineering (RCEnE) in conjunction with 7th International Conference of Thermofluids (THERMOFLUID). RCEnE provides a global platform to bring together researchers, scientists, academics, policy makers and professionals to exchange information and identify research needs in the areas of energy engineering. The annual THERMOFLUID focuses on the fields of fluid and thermal engineering and sciences. Conjoining these two conferences creates a forum for knowledge sharing of a wealth of information in both fundamental and applied sciences.

All accepted full papers will be published inThe AIP Conference Proceedings Journal indexed by Elsevier SCOPUS (www.scopus.com), and SJR (www.scimagojr.com) with an SJR index of 0.15 and H index of 34.
